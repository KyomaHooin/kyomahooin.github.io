Authors September 16 1997

Effinger, George Alec

o A Fire in the Sun (1989)
More entangling alliances shroud the existence of the Phoenix file --- an agreement between two rivals over the control of world-wide black markets. Sequel to When Gravity Fails.
o The Exile Kiss (1991)
The Hero and Kingpin introduced in `When Gravity Fails' are kid- napped, framed, and dropped off in the desert. Hero returns later to exact revenge.
o When Gravity Fails (1987)
A series of brutal murders prompts a king-pin of the post-superpower black market to adopt a police detective as a personal resource in an effort to find the deranged killer.

Ellison, Harlan

o I have No Mouth and I Must Scream
In this story, a large machine intellect has taken over the world -- has become the world -- and it keeps the few remaining humans living inside so that it can torment them. A very powerful story. - Charlie Stevens
