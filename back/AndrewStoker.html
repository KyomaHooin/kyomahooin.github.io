Disclaimer:

Be aware that this is a very large selection of books which may not all be CP but which do contain elements which lean in that direction. Some predate CP and other types of material are included which may fall on the fringe of the genre or may be closer to (post)apocalypse, or may otherwise interest you.
Things I might do: Scan in book covers as icons, say about 1 inch wide for an average paperback; include book details, ISBN and number of pages; scan video covers. Anyone like to make any other suggestions?

Needless to say, I'd also like additions to the list. I'll try and add new books that I read too.

If you're new(ish) to cyberpunk, and you really care about a total stranger's opinion, here's some of the following books I'd recommend as a starting point (some are out of print and might be hard to find though).
